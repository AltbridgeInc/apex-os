# Management Quality Assessment Skill

This skill enables evaluation of management team effectiveness through capital allocation decisions, shareholder alignment, strategic execution, and integrity.

**Assessment Areas**:
- Capital allocation track record (M&A, buybacks, dividends, R&D)
- Shareholder alignment (insider ownership, compensation structure)
- Strategic vision and execution
- Transparency and integrity
- Culture and people management

**Auto-loaded for**: fundamental-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 management score based on:
- ROIC on capital deployed
- Insider ownership percentage
- Guidance accuracy
- Communication quality
- Governance structure

**Red Flags Detected**:
- Accounting fraud or investigation
- Value-destructive M&A pattern
- High executive turnover
- Aggressive accounting
- Misaligned compensation

Agents with this skill automatically evaluate whether management can be trusted with investor capital.
