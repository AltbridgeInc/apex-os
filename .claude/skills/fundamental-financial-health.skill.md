# Financial Health Analysis Skill

This skill enables comprehensive financial health assessment of companies through systematic analysis of:
- Revenue trends and quality
- Profitability metrics and margins
- Cash flow generation and conversion
- Balance sheet strength and leverage

**Auto-loaded for**: fundamental-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 financial health score based on:
- Revenue growth consistency
- Margin trends
- Free cash flow generation
- Debt levels and coverage ratios

**Red Flags Detected**:
- Accounting quality issues
- Margin compression
- Cash flow divergence from earnings
- Dangerous debt levels

Agents with this skill automatically apply comprehensive financial analysis frameworks when evaluating investment opportunities.
