# Trend Identification Skill

This skill enables systematic identification of trend direction and strength across monthly, weekly, and daily timeframes to determine optimal trade alignment.

**Analysis Methods**:
- Multi-timeframe analysis (monthly/weekly/daily)
- Moving averages (20/50/200-day)
- Trendline analysis
- ADX trend strength measurement
- Price action structure (HH/HL or LH/LL)

**Auto-loaded for**: technical-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 trend score based on:
- Timeframe alignment (all in sync = highest)
- MA positioning and slopes
- ADX reading (>25 = strong)
- HH/HL pattern clarity
- Trend duration and consistency

**Trading Rules Applied**:
- Buy pullbacks in uptrends
- Avoid or short in downtrends
- Wait for breakouts in sideways
- Trend break triggers exit

Agents with this skill automatically assess trend quality and ensure trading with prevailing momentum.
