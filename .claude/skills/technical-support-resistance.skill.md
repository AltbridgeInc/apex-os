# Support & Resistance Skill

This skill enables identification of critical price levels where supply and demand dynamics create barriers, informing entry/exit timing and risk management.

**S/R Types Identified**:
- Horizontal S/R (swing highs/lows)
- Moving average S/R (dynamic levels)
- Trendline S/R (diagonal)
- Round numbers (psychological)
- Fibonacci retracements
- Volume profile (high/low volume nodes)

**Auto-loaded for**: technical-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 S/R score based on:
- Level clarity and strength
- Multiple confirmations
- Volume at levels
- Multiple timeframe visibility

**Trading Applications**:
- Entry at support zones
- Exit at resistance zones
- Stop placement below support
- Profit targets at resistance
- Role reversal after breaks

Agents with this skill automatically identify key levels for tactical entry/exit decisions and risk management.
