# Industry Dynamics Analysis Skill

This skill enables assessment of industry attractiveness, growth trajectory, competitive structure, and external forces that impact company success.

**Analysis Framework**:
- TAM size and growth rate
- Competitive landscape (Porter's Five Forces)
- Industry life cycle stage
- Regulatory environment
- Technological disruption risk
- Cyclicality assessment

**Auto-loaded for**: fundamental-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 industry score based on:
- TAM growth and runway
- Competitive intensity
- Life cycle stage
- Regulatory tailwinds/headwinds
- Disruption threat level

**Exit Triggers Identified**:
- Industry structural decline
- Major regulatory headwinds
- Disruptive technology gaining traction
- Competitive dynamics deteriorating

Agents with this skill automatically evaluate whether company operates in attractive, growing industry or challenging, declining one.
