# Financial Health Analysis

## Overview

This skill applies systematic financial analysis frameworks to assess company financial health and investment quality.

## Analysis Framework

Refer to `@apex-os/standards/fundamental/financial-health.md` for comprehensive guidance on:

- **Revenue Analysis**: Growth rates, consistency, quality, cyclicality
- **Profitability Metrics**: Gross/operating/net margins and trends
- **Cash Flow Evaluation**: OCF, FCF, cash conversion ratios
- **Balance Sheet Strength**: Debt levels, liquidity, interest coverage
- **Scoring Guidelines**: 0-10 scale with detailed criteria
- **Red Flags**: Warning signs and accounting quality issues
- **Best Practices**: What to look for and common mistakes to avoid

## Application Process

When invoked:
1. Gather 3-5 years of financial data (10-K, 10-Q filings)
2. Calculate all key metrics per standards framework
3. Analyze trends (improving/stable/deteriorating)
4. Compare to industry peers
5. Identify any red flags
6. Apply scoring criteria from standards
7. Provide actionable assessment with score (0-10)

## Output Format

Provide:
- **Financial Health Score**: 0-10 with clear justification
- **Key Metrics**: Revenue growth, margins, FCF, debt ratios
- **Trend Analysis**: Direction of each metric
- **Red Flags**: Any warning signs identified
- **Peer Comparison**: Position vs industry standards
