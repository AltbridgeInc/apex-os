# Competitive Moat Analysis Skill

This skill enables identification and assessment of sustainable competitive advantages that protect companies from competition.

**Moat Types Evaluated**:
- Network effects (strongest)
- Brand strength
- Cost advantages
- Switching costs
- Regulatory/IP protection

**Auto-loaded for**: fundamental-analyst agent

**Scoring Output**: 0-10 moat score based on:
- Type and strength of moat(s)
- ROIC sustainability
- Market share trends
- Competitive response effectiveness

**Durability Tests Applied**:
- Pricing power assessment
- Return on invested capital trends
- Market share trajectory
- Competitive attack resistance
- New entrant success rate

Agents with this skill automatically evaluate competitive positioning and moat durability when analyzing companies.
